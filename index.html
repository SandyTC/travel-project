Why Berlin?

Berlin, the city where anything is possible

Berlin, the capital city of Germany, is renowned for its exceptional range of landmarks, vibrant cultural scene and way of life that's somehow all go yet relaxed.

In fact, the city is best known for its striking contrasts. Historical buildings stand alongside modern architecture as the past and present intermingle. The sights Berlin has to offer, from the Brandenburg Gate to the Chancellor's Office, bear witness to the history of an entire nation.

Food

Great places to eat in Berlin

Ferri's Ristorante

An Italian restaurant, located on one of Europe's most famous shopping streets, opposite the Kaiser Wilhelm Memorial Church on Breitscheidplatz.

Address:

Rankestraße 1, 10789 Berlin, Germany

What I like about it

Classic Italian dishes at reasonable prices. I visited on my birthday in 2019, after visiting Berlin Zoo.

Tuans Hütte

An excellent restaurant offering both traditional Thai food as well as sushi, located 5 minutes walk from Alexanderplatz station.

Address:

Dircksenstraße 40, 10178 Berlin, Germany

What I like about it

Fantastic, freshly prepared sushi, I ordered a Tuan's Sushi Platter to share with a friend. Tasty and delicious.

Learn more

Mustafa's Gemüse Kebap

When in Berlin, you MUST have a döner kebab, the most famous one being Mustafa's. Be prepared to queue for a while!

Address:

Mehringdamm 32, 10961 Berlin, Germany

What I like about it

This is as authentic as it gets for a true German kebab, super-fresh ingredients, absolutely delicious!
